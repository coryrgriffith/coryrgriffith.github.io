A Little About Me

I am a former therapeutic recreation professional. I have always enjoyed puzzles and trying to slove them: whether the puzzle is in a video game, a math problem, or human behavior.

Through my work in special recreation, I assist individuals with disabilities to improve their lives in some way using recreation as my tool. I sometimes have to use my problem solving skills to figure out some challenging behaviors to better help an individual participate to their fullest potential.

Helping others is one of my biggest motivations in life. Being a part of someone else's success is just as, if not more important than my personal success.
